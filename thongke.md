# Chi tiet chuc nang thong ke Admin

## 1. Muc tieu

Trang thong ke Admin dung de theo doi tinh hinh san pham, ton kho, doanh thu, don hang va hieu qua ban hang.

Admin can nhin nhanh duoc:

- San pham nao dang ban tot
- San pham nao sap het hang
- San pham nao khong co nguoi mua
- Danh muc nao tao doanh thu tot
- Ma giam gia nao dang hieu qua
- Doanh thu va so luong ban thay doi theo ngay, tuan, thang

## 2. Thong ke tong quan

### 2.1 Tong so san pham

Hien thi tong so loai san pham dang co trong he thong.

Can co:

- Tong so san pham
- So san pham dang hien thi
- So san pham bi an
- So san pham het hang
- So san pham sap het hang

### 2.2 Tong doanh thu

Hien thi doanh thu tu cac don hang da thanh toan hoac da giao thanh cong.

Can co:

- Tong doanh thu hom nay
- Tong doanh thu trong 7 ngay
- Tong doanh thu trong thang
- Tong doanh thu theo khoang ngay tuy chon

### 2.3 Tong don hang

Hien thi so luong don hang theo trang thai.

Can co:

- Tong don hang
- Don cho xac nhan
- Don da xac nhan
- Don dang giao
- Don da giao
- Don da huy

## 3. Thong ke san pham

### 3.1 San pham ban chay nhat

Liet ke cac san pham co so luong ban ra cao nhat.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Hinh anh
- Danh muc
- So luong da ban
- Doanh thu tao ra
- Ton kho con lai

### 3.2 San pham ban cham nhat

Liet ke cac san pham co it hoac khong co don hang trong mot khoang thoi gian.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Ngay tao san pham
- Lan ban gan nhat
- So luong da ban
- Ton kho con lai

Muc dich:

- Biet san pham nao nen giam gia
- Biet san pham nao nen an khoi cua hang
- Biet san pham nao can doi hinh anh, mo ta hoac gia ban

### 3.3 San pham co doanh thu cao nhat

Liet ke san pham tao ra nhieu tien nhat, khong chi dua vao so luong ban.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Gia ban
- So luong da ban
- Tong doanh thu
- Ty le dong gop vao doanh thu chung

### 3.4 San pham duoc xem nhieu nhat

Theo doi san pham co nhieu luot xem.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- So luot xem
- So luong da ban
- Ty le chuyen doi tu xem sang mua

Muc dich:

- Neu san pham nhieu luot xem nhung it nguoi mua, can kiem tra lai gia, anh, mo ta hoac ton kho.

### 3.5 San pham duoc yeu thich nhieu nhat

Liet ke san pham duoc them vao yeu thich nhieu.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- So luot yeu thich
- So luong da ban
- Ton kho con lai

### 3.6 San pham duoc them vao gio nhieu nhat

Theo doi san pham duoc them vao gio hang nhieu.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- So lan them vao gio
- So don hang thanh cong
- Ty le tu gio hang sang mua hang

## 4. Thong ke ton kho

### 4.1 San pham het hang

Liet ke san pham co so luong ton kho bang 0.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Danh muc
- Ngay het hang neu co
- So luong da ban truoc do

### 4.2 San pham sap het hang

Liet ke san pham co ton kho thap hon muc canh bao.

Vi du:

- Ton kho <= 5
- Ton kho <= 10
- Hoac muc canh bao rieng theo tung san pham

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Ton kho hien tai
- So luong ban trong 7 ngay gan nhat
- Goi y nhap them

### 4.3 San pham ton kho cao nhung ban cham

Liet ke san pham con nhieu hang nhung ban it.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Ton kho hien tai
- So luong da ban
- So ngay khong co don moi

Muc dich:

- De xuat giam gia
- De xuat day len trang chu
- De xuat chay voucher

## 5. Thong ke theo danh muc

### 5.1 Danh muc ban chay nhat

Liet ke danh muc co so luong san pham ban ra cao nhat.

Can hien thi:

- Ten danh muc
- So san pham trong danh muc
- Tong so luong da ban
- Tong doanh thu

### 5.2 Danh muc doanh thu cao nhat

Liet ke danh muc tao ra doanh thu cao nhat.

Can hien thi:

- Ten danh muc
- Tong doanh thu
- So don hang lien quan
- San pham ban chay nhat trong danh muc

### 5.3 Danh muc ban cham

Liet ke danh muc co doanh thu thap hoac it don hang.

Can hien thi:

- Ten danh muc
- So san pham
- So luong da ban
- Doanh thu

## 6. Thong ke danh gia san pham

### 6.1 Diem danh gia trung binh

Theo doi chat luong san pham qua danh gia cua khach hang.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Diem danh gia trung binh
- Tong so danh gia
- So danh gia 1 sao, 2 sao, 3 sao, 4 sao, 5 sao

### 6.2 San pham co nhieu danh gia thap

Liet ke san pham co nhieu danh gia 1-2 sao.

Can hien thi:

- Ten san pham
- Diem trung binh
- So danh gia thap
- Noi dung danh gia gan nhat

Muc dich:

- Kiem tra chat luong san pham
- Kiem tra mo ta co dung thuc te khong
- Xu ly phan hoi khach hang

## 7. Thong ke ma giam gia

### 7.1 Ma giam gia dang hoat dong

Liet ke cac ma giam gia con hieu luc.

Can hien thi:

- Ma giam gia
- Kieu giam gia: phan tram hoac so tien
- Gia tri giam
- So luong da dung
- So luong con lai
- Ngay het han
- Trang thai

### 7.2 Ma giam gia sap het luot

Liet ke ma giam gia co so luong con lai thap.

Can hien thi:

- Ma giam gia
- So luong gioi han
- So luong da dung
- So luong con lai

### 7.3 Ma giam gia sap het han

Liet ke ma giam gia sap qua ngay het han.

Can hien thi:

- Ma giam gia
- Ngay het han
- So ngay con lai
- So luot da dung

### 7.4 Hieu qua ma giam gia

Theo doi ma nao tao ra nhieu don hang hoac doanh thu.

Can hien thi:

- Ma giam gia
- So don hang da ap dung
- Tong tien da giam
- Doanh thu sau giam

## 8. Bieu do can co

### 8.1 Bieu do doanh thu

Can co cac che do:

- Theo ngay
- Theo tuan
- Theo thang
- Theo khoang ngay tuy chon

### 8.2 Bieu do so luong san pham ban ra

Can hien thi:

- Tong so luong san pham ban ra theo thoi gian
- Co the loc theo danh muc
- Co the loc theo tung san pham

### 8.3 Bieu do don hang

Can hien thi:

- So don moi
- So don huy
- So don thanh cong
- So don dang giao

### 8.4 Bieu do ton kho

Can hien thi:

- San pham sap het hang
- San pham ton kho cao
- San pham het hang

## 9. Bo loc can co

Trang thong ke nen co bo loc de admin xem du lieu de hon.

Can co:

- Loc theo ngay
- Loc theo tuan
- Loc theo thang
- Loc theo khoang ngay tuy chon
- Loc theo danh muc
- Loc theo san pham
- Loc theo trang thai don hang
- Loc theo trang thai ton kho
- Loc theo ma giam gia

## 10. Canh bao quan trong

Trang thong ke nen co khu vuc canh bao nhanh.

Canh bao nen co:

- San pham sap het hang
- San pham da het hang
- San pham ton kho cao nhung ban cham
- San pham co nhieu danh gia thap
- Ma giam gia sap het han
- Ma giam gia sap het luot dung
- Don hang bi huy tang bat thuong

## 11. Uu tien trien khai

### Giai doan 1

Lam cac thong ke co ban truoc:

- Tong san pham
- Tong don hang
- Tong doanh thu
- San pham ban chay
- Ton kho hien tai
- San pham sap het hang
- Doanh thu theo ngay, tuan, thang

### Giai doan 2

Mo rong theo doi chi tiet hon:

- San pham ban cham
- San pham duoc xem nhieu
- San pham duoc yeu thich nhieu
- Thong ke theo danh muc
- Danh gia san pham

### Giai doan 3

Them phan phan tich nang cao:

- Ty le chuyen doi tu xem sang mua
- Ty le tu gio hang sang don hang
- Hieu qua ma giam gia
- Canh bao tu dong
- Goi y nhap hang hoac giam gia

## 12. Goi y hien thi giao dien

Trang Admin thong ke nen chia thanh cac khu vuc:

- Khu tong quan o dau trang
- Bieu do doanh thu va don hang
- Bang san pham ban chay
- Bang san pham sap het hang
- Bang danh muc ban chay
- Bang ma giam gia
- Khu canh bao

Nen uu tien bang so lieu ro rang, de xem nhanh, khong nen lam giao dien qua roi.
